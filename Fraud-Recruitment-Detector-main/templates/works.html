02 : Works

Online Fraud Recruitment is the problem faced by any job seeker. In order to solve this problem we have made the machine learning model which can detect the probability of a job to be fake or not. Here is our System Architecture which is further divided into 6 phases.

001/006

Data Selection

Employment Scam Aegean Dataset(EMSCAD) datset which is freely available on kaggle contains 17,880 real-life job postings in which 17,014 are real and 866 are fake.
